In Endava, many of us are able to focus on our usual priorities and routines. For some of our colleagues, especially in and around the Middle East, life looks and feels very different right now.



Our teams in the region are living and working through an active conflict situation. They are continuing to show remarkable commitment and professionalism, but their safety and well-being must come first. I’m asking all of us to hold that in mind over the coming days and weeks.



If you work with colleagues currently in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Saudi Arabia, Doha or nearby locations, please look for ways to lighten their load where you can – whether that is being flexible on deadlines, taking on an extra task, or simply making space for them to step back when they need to.



I also encourage you to reach out personally. A short message that says “I’m thinking of you – how are you and your family doing?” can mean far more than we realise.
